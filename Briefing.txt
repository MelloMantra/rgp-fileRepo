 In the folder of any given pack, you will 
 find 2 things: a Navigator program and a  
 README.txt. You can ignore the "Contents"  
 folder, as everything you need can be found  
 outside of it. The README will go over what is 
 included and how everything in the pack 
 works. 

 To proceed, replace the true1 in the  
 EULA1.txt to true2.
